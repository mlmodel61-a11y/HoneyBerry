About HoneyBerry

A caring Kumasi school for early learning and lower primary growth.

HoneyBerry International School exists to help young children feel safe, confident, curious, and ready for strong foundational learning.

Our story

Built for parents who want care, structure, and a warm start.

HoneyBerry International School was founded to provide families in Kumasi with a dependable early learning environment: clear routines, caring supervision, responsive administration, and teachers who know the children in their care.

Over the years, the school has grown through parent referrals and steady trust. We continue to invest in teacher development, classroom resources, reading culture, creative work, and practical activities that make learning active and memorable.

Principal's message

Parents should feel their child is known, safe, and supported.

At HoneyBerry, we believe strong early learning is built through partnership. Our teachers work hard to make each day calm, purposeful, and engaging, while our administration keeps communication open with parents.

We welcome families who want a school that values reading, numbers, creativity, manners, play, and the full development of each child.
