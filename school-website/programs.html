Programs

A gentle learning pathway from Play Group to Class 3.

Each stage is designed with age-appropriate care, playful discovery, foundational skills, and close teacher attention.

Play Group

Gentle Settling and Sensory Play

A warm first school experience with patient care, movement, songs, and supervised discovery.

  • Safe settling and familiar routines
  • Sensory play and movement
  • Language stimulation through songs
Crèche

Safe Care and Gentle Routines

Children receive attentive supervision, simple routines, rest, play, and early social support.

  • Caring supervision and comfort
  • Simple routines and social play
  • Early confidence and independence
Nursery

Language, Play, and Confidence

Children build vocabulary, motor skills, independence, and social confidence through guided activities.

  • Stories, songs, and conversation
  • Fine motor and sensory activities
  • Art, movement, and supervised play
Kindergarten

Readiness and Early Skills

Structured play, early reading, numeracy readiness, creativity, and confidence prepare children for Class 1.

  • Phonics and pre-reading skills
  • Number sense and patterns
  • Art, music, movement, and play
Class 1-3

Lower Primary Foundations

Children strengthen reading, writing, number work, creativity, problem solving, and classroom confidence.

  • Reading, writing, and comprehension
  • Practical numeracy and class projects
  • Gentle feedback and parent updates
Learning by doingChildren learn through guided practice, stories, art, play, and teacher interaction.

How we teach

Structured learning that still feels warm and engaging.

Our teachers combine clear instruction with songs, stories, demonstrations, hands-on activities, group work, and individual support.

  • Age-appropriate weekly lesson planning.
  • Reading, numeracy, creativity, and practical activities.
  • Support for children who need extra encouragement.
  • Values-led routines that promote kindness, confidence, and respect.

Find the right class

Speak with admissions about the best stage for your child.

We can advise on age, readiness, daily routines, school records where needed, and class availability.
