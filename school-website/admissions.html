Admission process

Simple steps from enquiry to enrolment.

We keep the process clear so parents can make decisions quickly and confidently.

Enquiry

Contact the school by phone, WhatsApp, or the contact form to confirm the class you are interested in.

School visit

Tour the campus, meet the admissions team, and discuss your child's age, routine, and readiness.

Readiness chat

For Kindergarten and Lower Primary, we use a gentle interaction to understand your child's current learning stage.

Enrolment

Submit documents, complete registration, receive the school information pack, and prepare for a smooth start.

Requirements

What parents should prepare.

Requirements may vary by class level, but most admissions require the items below.

  • Completed admission form.
  • Child's birth certificate or national identification record where available.
  • Two recent passport-size photographs.
  • Previous school report for children entering Lower Primary, where available.
  • A gentle readiness interaction for Kindergarten and Lower Primary children.
  • Parent or guardian contact details for school communication.
